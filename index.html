About Me

Hi, I'm Adanna! I'm a newly graduated software engineer with a background in biology and healthcare. Following my lifelong love of learning and problem solving has led me to pursue a career in software engineering, where I am constantly faced with new challenges and opportunities to learn and grow. I thrive in collaborative settings, recognizing the value of diverse perspectives and the power of teamwork. I aspire to make a meaningful impact with my work and help make technology accessible to all.

In my free time, I enjoy rock climbing, spending time in nature, making art, and dabbling in video games. I'm also a dedicated butler for 2 adorable cats!
